Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the packages?

All packages include accommodation, meals, guided activities, cultural experiences, and a tree-planting ceremony. Transportation from the starting point is also included.

What should I bring?

Comfortable walking shoes, sun protection, light clothing for daytime and warmer layers for evenings, camera, reusable water bottle, and an open mind!

Is this suitable for solo travelers?

Absolutely! Many of our guests travel solo. You'll join a small group and make lasting friendships while experiencing Botswana's culture together.

How do I make payment?

After submitting your booking request, we'll contact you with payment options including bank transfer, PayPal, or Orange Money. A deposit of 30% secures your booking.
