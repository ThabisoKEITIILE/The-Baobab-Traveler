Full Itinerary

Day 1: Welcome & Cultural Introduction
  • Arrival at Mathangwane Village
  • Traditional welcome ceremony with local community
  • Introduction to village life and customs
  • Sunset walk to nearby baobab grove
  • Welcome dinner with traditional dishes
  • Evening storytelling around the fire
Day 2: Eco-Walks & Conservation
  • Morning nature walk with local guide
  • Learn about native plants and their traditional uses
  • Visit to sustainable agriculture project
  • Lunch with ingredients from local gardens
  • Tree-planting ceremony (your baobab sapling!)
  • Documentary about Botswana's ecosystem
  • Traditional dinner and cultural music performance
Day 3: Culinary & Craft Experiences
  • Early market visit to source ingredients
  • Traditional cooking class (seswaa and morogo)
  • Communal lunch with village families
  • Afternoon basket weaving workshop
  • Visit to local pottery artisans
  • Farewell dinner prepared by you and the group
  • Certificate presentation ceremony
Day 4: Reflection & Departure
  • Sunrise meditation under the baobabs
  • Breakfast and packing
  • Group photo session
  • Visit your planted tree one last time
  • Farewell ceremony with community
  • Transfer to departure point
